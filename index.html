FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get a technician?
We offer same-day service in most cities. You can book a slot as early as 2 hours from now. Our technicians are available 7 AM to 10 PM, all 7 days of the week including holidays.
Are your technicians verified and trained?
Yes. Every EZAC technician undergoes a thorough background verification (Aadhaar-linked, police verification), technical training, and brand-specific certification before being onboarded. We regularly re-train our team on new models.
What is the 90-day service warranty?
If the same issue returns within 90 days of our service, we will fix it at absolutely no extra cost. This applies to all repair services (not diagnostic-only visits). Our AMC plan extends this to 1 full year.
How is pricing decided? Do you charge for the visit?
The Basic service includes a ₹299 inspection/diagnosis fee. If you proceed with the repair, that fee is waived or adjusted against the total cost. Full pricing is shown upfront — you approve before we start any work. No surprises.
Which brands and appliances do you service?
We service all major brands including LG, Samsung, Voltas, Daikin, Hitachi, Godrej, Whirlpool, Haier, Panasonic, O'General, Blue Star, and more — for ACs, refrigerators, washing machines, geysers, and other appliances.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, UPI (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm), debit/credit cards, and net banking. You pay only after the service is completed and you are satisfied. No advance payment required for most services.
